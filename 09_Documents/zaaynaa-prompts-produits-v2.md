## 🎯 BRAND RULES (à coller en tête de CHAQUE prompt)

```
I'm creating jewelry product images for ZAAYNAA — a "Modern Moroccan Quiet Luxury"
fine jewelry brand. The aesthetic is a precise blend of THREE worlds:
1. MODERN — Parisian minimalism, clean lines, Mejuri-thin proportions, editorial.
2. LUXURY — Dior Joaillerie mood, museum-quality lighting, refined, never cheap.
3. ARAB & MOROCCAN CULTURE — hidden zellige geometry, arabic calligraphy, riad
   light, lanterns, mint tea, dried botanicals — subtle, discovered slowly.

Rules:
- Soft natural OR warm cinematic light only. No harsh/dramatic black backgrounds.
- Thin, feminine, refined proportions. Never oversized, never bridal-traditional.
- Moroccan details are HIDDEN — a whisper, not a shout ("discret mais WOW de près").
- Materials: sterling silver 925 + 18k gold vermeil. Arabic calligraphy must be clean.
- Photorealistic, high-end e-commerce + editorial. Keep the ring identical to reference.
```

## 🔧 Astuces
- Trop chargé / trop « bijou de mariée » → ajouter « more delicate, Mejuri-thin, minimal ».
- Détail marocain pas visible → « show the hidden moroccan zellige detail more clearly in macro ».
- Lumière trop dure → « softer warm light, luminous, not dramatic ».
- Calligraphie déformée → « clean, correct arabic calligraphy, sharp engraving ».
- Générer la story card à part si le texte sort mal (la faire ensuite sur Canva avec le logo).
